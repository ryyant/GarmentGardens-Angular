Sustainable apparel are much harder to sell, given the multitude of fast fashion options available. Companies producing sustainable apparel struggle in terms of generating ROI due to the low profit margin. Meanwhile, patrons who frequent sustainable clothing indicate that shopping for such apparel is a tedious process, due to the lack of a centralised platform. Garment gardens aim to bridge this gap and make sustainable clothing convenient to purchase, and easy to sell.
